County Election Results 4 May 2017

Published: Fri 05 May 2017
Updated: Sat 09 May 2020
By stevehemingway

In 2017.

Elections in North Herts

Congratulations to Michael Muir, Judi Billing, David Barnard, Derek Ashley, Richard Thake, Simon Bloxham, Terry Hone, Fiona Hill and Steve Jarvis for winning County Council seats. Also congratulations to Ray Shakespeare-Smith and Sarah Dingley in District bye-elections. For full results in North Herts visit the NHDC website.
